Things to Do in Spiti Valley

Spiti Valley is a paradise for nature lovers, photographers, and adventure seekers. From rugged terrain and ancient monasteries to stargazing and river rafting, Spiti offers a bit of everything. Visit local homes, sip on butter tea, and hike among the most majestic peaks in India.

Best Time to Visit

The valley is best visited between May and October when roads are accessible and the weather is pleasant. Winter (November to March) transforms Spiti into a snow wonderland, but many routes may be closed due to heavy snowfall.

Spiti Culture & Travel Tips

Spiti's people follow Tibetan Buddhism. Expect monasteries, prayer flags, and warm hospitality. Local cuisine includes momos, thukpa, yak butter tea, and barley dishes.

  • Acclimatize for altitude sickness (AMS).
  • BSNL and Jio work best in the region.
  • Respect locals and avoid using plastic.
  • Carry power banks, jackets, and dry snacks.

Welcome to Dikit-Ling Eco Lodge

Perched in the tranquil high-altitude hamlet of Kibber Village in the mystical Spiti Valley, Dikit-Ling Eco Lodge invites you to experience the raw, untouched beauty of the Himalayas. Located at an elevation of over 14,000 feet, our lodge is surrounded by majestic mountains, stark valleys, and one of the clearest night skies in the world ‚Äî offering an unforgettable retreat for adventurers, peace-seekers, and culture enthusiasts alike.

Built using traditional Spitian stone and mud architecture, Dikit-Ling Eco Lodge is a harmonious blend of local craftsmanship and sustainable living. We believe in preserving the fragile ecosystem of the cold desert by embracing eco-friendly practices such as solar power, minimal plastic usage, and locally sourced produce.

Our lodge offers a selection of thoughtfully designed rooms, each providing panoramic views of the surrounding mountains, open valleys, and the ancient village below. Whether you're relaxing with a cup of butter tea or preparing for a trek to the nearby Kanamo Peak, the atmosphere here is peaceful, grounding, and rejuvenating.

The food at Dikit-Ling is a highlight in itself. Our in-house kitchen serves traditional Spitian meals alongside popular Indian and continental options ‚Äî all made with love, simplicity, and local ingredients.

Whether you're visiting in summer for the vibrant landscapes or braving the winter cold to witness the stark beauty of snow-covered Spiti, our team is here to make your journey unforgettable. With warm hospitality and meaningful experiences, Dikit-Ling Eco Lodge is not just a place to stay ‚Äî it's a place to belong.
